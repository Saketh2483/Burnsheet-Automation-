# Hours Rules

## SOW Stream & Talent Type Hours Mapping

| SOW Stream | Talent Type | Hours Rule |
|------------|-------------|-----------|
| Onshore SOW | Onshore Talent (USA/Canada) | 168 hrs/month |
| Onshore SOW | Offshore Talent billed under Onshore | 189 hrs/month |
| India SOW | India Talent only | 176 hrs/month |
| BTM | BTM Talent | 168 hrs/month |

## Automatic Calculation

**Monthly Burn = Rate Card × Monthly Hours**

- **Rate Card**: Hourly rate of the resource
- **Monthly Hours**: Determined by SOW Stream and Talent Type (from table above)
- **Monthly Burn**: Total monthly cost for the resource
